File formatting is a very important part of ADA compliance. Any file that is downloaded, be it transcripts or something similar, needs to be readily usable by a screen reader. There are several tools used to help this process. Not only do the files need to be screen reader accessable, but they also need to meet several other guidelines. Presentation is more than just making your website attractive for your users. It is also necessary for accessibility. Many people are color blind or have difficulites making out text that does not contrast enough. Presentation also encapsulates the structure of your website. With a poor structure, someone using a screen reader could easily get lost on your website. Click the button below to head over to our page on presentation.

Many people who use screen readers or those who are visually impared will be navigating your website with a keyboard. Key trapping is an issue of which you need to be mindful. This commonly happens when the user gets to a form field and is unable to use the keyboard to get out. How do you prevent people from becoming stuck on your website (key trapping) when they navbigate with a keyboard?
